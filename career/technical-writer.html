Technical writer

Back to current openings

We are looking for technical writer to document concepts, architectures and procedures around the developed application.
The successful candidate will work independently and collaborate with developers, SMEs, and stakeholders to meet deadlines.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Prepare, maintain and update technical documentation including description of application architecture, infrastructure, DB schemas, algorithms etc.

  • Collaborate with technical staff and SMEs.

  • Work with Jira, Confluence etc. to collect information.

MANDATORY SKILLS

  • Proven experience as a technical writer working with enterprise-level software development

  • 3-5 years English language documentation authoring

  • Expert in creating clear, concise language and sentence structure

  • Template design experience

  • Experience in API documentation

  • Experience in Confluence, Office 365, and SharePoint

  • Ability to research and collate background information from a variety of internal and external sources

  • Writing samples required

  • Able to focus on detail whilst explaining the big picture

NICE-TO-HAVE SKILLS

  • A knowledge of Agile methodologies

  • Experience in AWS or other Cloud technologies
